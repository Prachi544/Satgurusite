Why Namkaran Sanskar Matters

Namkaran Sanskar is a sacred Hindu naming ceremony performed to officially give a newborn their name with divine blessings.

It marks the child's introduction to family, society, and spiritual traditions. The ceremony is conducted to seek good health, happiness, prosperity, and a bright future while invoking the blessings of the deities for the child's well-being.

विधि

How the Ceremony Unfolds

Step 1

Sankalp

The family takes a sacred vow and states the purpose of performing the Namkaran Sanskar.

Step 2

Ganesh Pooja

Lord Ganesha is worshipped to remove obstacles and bless the child.

Step 3

Kalash Sthapana

The sacred Kalash is established, and prayers are offered to invoke divine blessings.

Step 4

Naming Ceremony

The chosen name is whispered into the baby's ear and formally announced before the family.

Step 5

Blessings by Elders

Family members and elders bless the child for a healthy, happy, and prosperous life.

Step 6

Aarti & Prasad

The ceremony concludes with aarti, followed by the distribution of prasad and celebrations.
