Why Havan Matters

Havan is a sacred Vedic fire ritual performed to invoke divine blessings, purify the surroundings, and create a positive spiritual atmosphere. Offerings are made into the holy fire while chanting Vedic mantras to promote peace, prosperity, health, and well-being. It is commonly performed during auspicious occasions, festivals, housewarming ceremonies, and other important life events.

विधि

How the Ceremony Unfolds

Step 1

Sankalp (Sacred Resolution)

The devotee states the purpose of performing the Havan.

Step 2

Ganesh Pooja

Lord Ganesha is worshipped to remove obstacles and ensure a successful ritual.

Step 3

Home Blessing

XThe sacred fire is lit and invoked as the divine messenger.

Step 4

Ahuti (Offerings)

Ghee, herbs, and other sacred materials are offered into the fire while chanting mantras.

Step 5

Purnahuti

The final offering is made to complete the Havan and seek divine blessings.

Step 6

Aarti & Prasad

The ritual concludes with aarti, followed by distributing prasad among devotees.
